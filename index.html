le jardin


Series of photos shot with my phone in my family garden. We moved into this house in 2007 and it has been under construction ever since. The work on the house is finished, but the garden is still unpolished and contains remnants of the construction era of the house. For a long time I thought that my house was not presentable due to its unfinished aspect. This garden where nature coexists with raw and industrial materials is a home for unfinished work.

CARD GAME


Here is a classic card game that I have designed. All collage illustrations were made using Stylist magazine (a free French magazine I used to distribute on the street). There is nothing special about this fashion magazine, apart from the fact that it has more ads than anything else. I expressed this vapidness in the numbers and characters. Apart from enjoying the purely materialistic, I also explored some of the themes associated with card suits: love, death, money and wealth.

Platja is the word for beach in Valenciano, from the Spanish region of Comunidad Valenciana where I used to go every summer. Having in mind the languor of beach sessions, I wanted to create a detached and carnal scenery.

Acrylic on canvas - 2020 - 38 x 41 cm

Assassins d'eau douce


This project is a book translation of an essay film: Freshwater assassins by French movie director Jean Painlevé. I used screenshots of the film to make collage illustrations, and after translating the scripts I cut through the text. This book is entirely waterproof. The cover has no title, it is made out of latex. I chose this material to get close to a slimy texture and at the same time a little bit inappropriate, as in the film.
